Latitude vs. Wind Speed


In this project, we used the OpenWeather API to request data on the weather conditions in selected cities.

On this particular day, wind speeds were low. The majority of cities had winds under 15 mph. There was a small trend when you got to the larger latitudes more occurences of high wind speed. When doing doing research, we discovered the Coriolis effect:" is zilch at the equator and increases with latitude to a maximum at the poles. Consequently, for a given wind speed, the Coriolis force will be stronger at higher latitudes than at lower latitudes."
